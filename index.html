What I do

Editing

Crafting powerful narratives through precise video editing and storytelling rhythm. I use tools like Premiere Pro and Avid Media Composer to cut, color, and shape footage into emotionally engaging visual experiences.

Compositing

Bringing visual elements together seamlessly to create stunning, photorealistic scenes. With Nuke and After Effects, I specialize in green screen removal, cleanup, rotoscoping, and high-end VFX integration for film and digital media.

Simulation

Creating dynamic simulations like smoke, fire, particles, destruction, and fluid FX using Houdini. These complex effects are tailored to add realism and cinematic impact to any visual project.

3D Animation

Designing impactful animations using Blender — from 3D motion to visual storytelling, enhancing every frame with energy and intent.

Works

Here are some of my favorite pieces from the project I did.

Chotta Mumbai | Trailer

Chotta Mumbai (transl. Little Mumbai) is a 2007 Indian Malayalam-language action comedy film directed by Anwar Rasheed, written by Benny P. Nayarambalam, and co-produced by Maniyanpilla Raju. The film stars Mohanlal in the lead role with Kalabhavan Mani,Siddique, Jagathy Sreekumar, Indrajith Sukumaran, Manikuttan, Bijukuttan, Sai Kumar, and Bhavana in supporting roles. Rahul Raj composed the score and songs of the film. The plot follows Vasco da Gama (Thala) and his gang of friends, all unemployed and leading a happy-go-lucky life until they encounter a corrupt police officer and gangster, Nadeshan.

L2E Empuraan | Malayalam Trailer

L2: Empuraan (transl. Overlord) stylised as L2: E.M.P.U.R.A.A.N marketed as L2E is a 2025 Indian Malayalam-language action thriller film directed by Prithviraj Sukumaran and written by Murali Gopy.

Kunjiramayanam Horror Trailer

Kunjiramayanam (transl. The story of Kunjiraman) is a 2015 Indian Malayalam-language comedy film directed by debutant Basil Joseph and scripted by Deepu Pradeep.The film features Vineeth Sreenivasan, Dhyan Sreenivasan,Aju Varghese, Neeraj Madhav and Deepak Parambol in the lead roles. Kunjiramayanam was released on 28 August 2015.
